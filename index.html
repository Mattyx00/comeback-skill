Features
Three ways to stay in sync
🎯 on by default

Focus

When Claude stops, your window snaps back to the foreground automatically. No more alt-tabbing to check if it's done.

focus: true
🔔 opt-in

Ring

Play a system sound when Claude finishes. Works even when you're in another app or have headphones on.

ring: true
▶︎ beta

YouTube sync

Playing music while you wait? The skill pauses your video when Claude needs you, resumes when you start working again.

youtube: true
How it works
Up in 30 seconds
1

Install the skill

Run npx skills add Mattyx00/comeback-skill in your project. Hooks are registered automatically in .claude/settings.json.

2

Configure with /comeback

Type /comeback in Claude Code. A short wizard lets you pick which features to enable and the scope (global or per-project).

3

Work, then come back

Let Claude do its thing. When it stops, you'll know — your window is focused, a sound plays, or your music pauses. Exactly what you configured.

Ready to stop tab-switching?
